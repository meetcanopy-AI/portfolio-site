Social graphics, Canva templates

Weekly Social Media System

A reusable content system for a service business that needed cleaner weekly posts, stronger offer visibility, and less design friction every time it published.

The problem

The business had useful content ideas, but every post looked like a separate design decision. The feed had no rhythm, the offer was not obvious, and posting took too long.

What I created

  • 12 Instagram feed graphics
  • 6 story templates
  • 3 carousel cover styles
  • Offer and testimonial graphic templates
  • Editable Canva files organized by content type

Outcome: a repeatable weekly system that gives the brand enough structure to post faster without making every graphic look the same.

Mini brand identity, social templates

Brand Starter Kit

A compact brand kit for a local service business that needed a more professional look before pushing its offer online.

The problem

The business had a real offer, but the visuals looked improvised. Colors, fonts, and layouts were being chosen one post at a time, which made the brand feel less established than it was.

What I created

  • Color palette with primary and accent uses
  • Typography direction for headlines and body copy
  • Simple logo usage guidance
  • 1-page mini brand guide
  • 8 Instagram and story templates

Outcome: a cleaner foundation that made the business look more credible before sending traffic to its offer.
